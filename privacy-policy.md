# Privacy Policy for Paper Chess

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Overview

Paper Chess ("we," "us," "our," or "App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and use.

## Data Collection

**Paper Chess does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

The App:
- Does not require login or account creation
- Does not track user behavior
- Does not collect location data
- Does not access device contacts, files, or photos
- Does not use analytics or crash reporting
- Does not contain ads or third-party tracking

## Local Data

All game data (board state, moves, player preferences) is stored **locally on your device only**. This data is never sent to our servers or third parties.

## Permissions

The App requests no special permissions beyond what's necessary to run the game.

## Third-Party Services

Paper Chess does not use any third-party SDKs or services that collect data.

## Children's Privacy

Paper Chess is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We'll notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
